Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for a visa application?

Required documents vary by destination and visa type. Generally, a valid passport, photos, application form, and supporting financial/travel documents are needed. We provide a detailed list for your specific case.

How long does the visa application process take?

Processing times depend on the embassy/consulate, visa type, and your nationality. We strive to expedite the process and will provide an estimated timeline upon consultation.

Can I track my visa application status?

Yes, we provide updates on your application status. Our team will keep you informed at every major milestone.

What if my visa application is denied?

While we aim for 100% success, in rare cases of denial, we will guide you through the next steps, including options for reapplication or appeal, based on the embassy's feedback.
